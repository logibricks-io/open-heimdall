## The Shift

Every six weeks, the frontier moves.

A new model arrives — faster, cheaper, more capable than the one before. The future quietly rewrites itself. And somewhere in the world, ten thousand applications written against last quarter's model begin their slow descent into obsolescence.

This is the central tension of the AI age. The substrate of intelligence now evolves faster than anything humans have ever built upon. Applications, by their nature, want to stand still. Models, by their nature, will not let them.

Heimdall stands at this fault line.

## The Thesis

> *Applications should not know which model they run on.*

They should know what they need — reasoning, vision, dialogue, code, judgment — and trust that the gate will provide it.

The model layer must be free to evolve at its own velocity. The application layer must be free to evolve at its own. Between them lies a membrane — a guardian who speaks both languages and serves both worlds.

When the next frontier model ships, your application will not learn of it. It will simply become better.

## What We Are Building

A single gate. To every model that exists, and every model that will.

Behind one OpenAI-compatible endpoint live six instruments, each named for a relic of Asgard, each answering an ancient question of the AI stack:

> **Bifröst** — *Where shall this request travel?*  
> **Aegis** — *How shall I shield what I serve?*  
> **Sleipnir** — *How shall I never run short of strength?*  
> **Forge** — *How shall I sharpen the words I send?*  
> **Gjallarhorn** — *What stirs across my realm?*  
> **Compass** — *What is the cost of all this?*

You may study each in our [documentation](https://github.com/logibricks-io/open-heimdall/tree/main/docs). Or you may simply point your application at Heimdall and forget the questions were ever yours to answer.

## What We Will Not Compromise

**Stability over novelty.** Your applications were not built to be rewritten every quarter. They will not be.

**Transparency over magic.** Every routing decision, every token, every cent — observable, attributable, replayable. No black boxes between you and your bill.

**Compatibility over invention.** We do not ask you to learn a new API. We speak the language you already speak.

**Openness over enclosure.** This is *Open* Heimdall. The gate is yours — to read, to run, to fork, to forge. Infrastructure of this consequence belongs in the commons.

## Begin

```bash
# Pull and run
docker run -d -p 8080:8080 ghcr.io/logibricks-io/open-heimdall:latest

# Or build from source
git clone https://github.com/logibricks-io/open-heimdall.git
cd open-heimdall && make run
```

Point your application at `http://localhost:8080/v1`.

Continue building.
